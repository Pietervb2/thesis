The first one concerns the temperature at the outlet of an edge at timestep k, $T_e[k]$. The Node Method (NM) is not specified for a mass flow rate of zero, in such case I only make use of the exponential relation between the water mass temperature and the ambient temperature, which is the last step of the Node Method.   

\begin{equation}
T_e[k] =
\begin{cases}
\text{only exp}, & \dot{m}_e[k] = 0 \\
\text{NM}, & \dot{m}_e[k] > 0 
\end{cases}
\end{equation}
